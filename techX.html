What is TechX?

TechX Sri Lanka 2025 is a flagship global event organized under the IEEE Computer Society Student & Young Professionals (SYP) program. This year, the event is proudly hosted by the IEEE Computer Society of Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, marking the first time a university in Sri Lanka has taken the lead in organizing TechX.

The event features a combination of tech-focused sessions, mentoring programs, and a national-level Ideahackathon competition. Designed to mirror global standards, TechX Sri Lanka connects students, professionals, and experts from across the country to learn, compete, and collaborate in the world of technology.

TechX Sri Lanka 2025 is open to all university students across Sri Lanka. Participants must register through the official event platform.
You can join as:
  • A competitor in the Ideahackathon (open to all universities)
  • An attendee for tech sessions and mentoring programs
  • A volunteer supporting the event's organization and success
No IEEE membership is required to participate in TechX Sri Lanka 2025.
